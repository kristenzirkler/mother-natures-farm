Visit the farm for a day in the snow!

Mother Nature's Farm annual snow day is Saturday December 9th, 2017 from 9:00am to 6:00pm. Bring your mittens and a snow tube, grab your friends and neighbors to come join the fun at Mother Nature's Farm.

{% include image-box.html layout="content float-right border blue" src="photo-snowday-1.jpg" title="Snow Day at Mother Nature's Farm" %}

Kids are $10.00 that includes the snow hill all day, the hayride, maze, air bounce and the animals, the adults are $5.00 to tag along. Maneau's Munchies will be open on the weekends for snack food, etc hotdogs, popcorn, cotton candy, chips, soda and hot drinks.

We have fresh cut Douglas Firs and Noble Firs for sale, and of course our live Elderica pines are here for you to cut down or have dug up for a really fresh, old fashioned Christmas Tree. Bring the family for the hunt for the perfect tree for your holidays!

{% include image-box.html layout="content float-left border blue" src="photo-snowday-2.jpg" title="Snow Day at Mother Nature's Farm" %}

We are open from 3:00 to 9:00 Monday through Thursday, and Friday, Saturday & Sunday we are open from 9:00 to 9:00.

Please note that we close at 3:00 on Christmas Eve.
